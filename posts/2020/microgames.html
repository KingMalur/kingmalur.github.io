Introducing MicroGames!

Hey, I heard you like side projects! So I started a side project while you were working on a side project so you can side project while you side project..

That’s what happened to me. Constantly.

I repeatedly wanted to try something new that I became interested in while doing some other project. I could focus on the new project but after a while it became boring. Nothing new happended and it would become too boring to create a full project out of it.

Well, that’s in the past! - But what are microGames? Good question!

They are ultra small games with a development time of under 10 hours and only core gameplay.

I hope that I can stick to a game given this short amount of time and eventually get better managing bigger games!

Be sure to follow my progress on GitLab.
